üî¨ TDI Assessment Details

TDI Questions: 10 sections covering thoracic-specific activities

üìù TDI Question Categories:

  1. Pain Intensity - Current thoracic/mid-back pain level
  2. Personal Care - Washing, dressing, daily hygiene
  3. Lifting - Ability to lift various weights
  4. Walking - Walking distances and limitations
  5. Sitting - Sitting tolerance and positioning
  6. Standing - Standing endurance and comfort
  7. Sleeping - Sleep quality and pain interference
  8. Work Activities - Job tasks and housework capacity
  9. Recreation - Leisure and recreational activities
  10. Breathing & Coughing - Respiratory function impact

Scoring: 0-50 points (0-5 per section), converted to percentage for SRS calculation

Status: Clinic-developed assessment, validation in progress
